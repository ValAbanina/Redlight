FAQs

Find answers to your most pressing questions about our Red Light Therapy devices.

What is Red Light Therapy?

Red Light Therapy is a non-invasive treatment that uses low-level red light wavelengths to penetrate the skin and stimulate cellular energy production. This process helps improve various health conditions and promotes overall wellness.

How does Red Light Therapy work?

Red light therapy works by emitting specific wavelengths of light that penetrate the skin and are absorbed by mitochondria in cells. This stimulates ATP production, which helps cells function more efficiently and promotes healing and regeneration.

What are the benefits of Red Light Therapy?

Red light therapy offers numerous benefits including improved skin health, reduced inflammation, enhanced muscle recovery, better sleep quality, increased energy levels, and pain relief. It's also used for anti-aging and general wellness purposes.

Is Red Light Therapy safe?

Yes, red light therapy is generally considered safe when used properly. It's non-invasive, doesn't produce heat, and doesn't cause burns. However, it's important to follow manufacturer guidelines and consult with a healthcare professional if you have specific medical conditions.

How often should I use Red Light Therapy?

The frequency of use depends on your specific goals and the device you're using. Generally, sessions can range from 3-5 times per week for 10-20 minutes each. It's best to start with shorter sessions and gradually increase as your body adapts.
